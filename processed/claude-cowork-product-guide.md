# The Claude Cowork Product Guide

**Source:** Anthropic. Claude Cowork — the knowledge work agent in the Claude desktop app.

## What Makes Claude Cowork Different

- **Local file access** — reads and writes files on your machine
- **Subagents and long-running work** — delegates multi-step tasks, runs in the background
- **Cross-app knowledge work** — connects to Slack, Google Drive, and other apps
- **Citations** — delivers output with references back to actual files and messages
- **You describe the goal**, Claude lays out the steps and delivers

## Product Matrix

| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| **Chat** | Conversational drafting, brainstorming |
| **Claude Code** | Coding and software engineering |
| **Claude Cowork** | Cross-app knowledge work |

## Seven Common Workflows

- Research briefs
- Meeting prep
- Recurring reports
- Cross-app data compilation
- Document generation from multiple sources
- Scheduled task automation
- Plugin-powered marketing and product management workflows

## Key Insight

> Claude Cowork flips the model: instead of bringing your work to Claude, you bring Claude to your work. Point it at a folder, connect your apps, describe the outcome, and step away.
