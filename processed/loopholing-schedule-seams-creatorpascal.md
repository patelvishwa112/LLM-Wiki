# Loopholing — Schedule Seams for Side Work

**Source:** [X Article @creatorpascal](https://x.com/creatorpascal/status/2075357304690516005)

## Summary

Monk-mode advice assumes you own the calendar. People with jobs and families get **pre-sold hours**. **Loopholing** reclaims only the **seams** between claimed blocks—commute, pre-meeting minutes, lunch, queues, post-bedtime quiet—while leaving job, family, and sleep sacred.

**Why seams beat free Saturdays:** (1) compression (Parkinson reverse), (2) instant-start muscle, (3) high-signal proof under constraint. **Audit:** map blocks → hunt seams (cross-check screen time) → assign missions night-before → stage tools for <10s start → guard sacred blocks harder. Math: ~80 min/day × 90 days ≈ **120 hours** (three work weeks) without enlarging the calendar.

Origin story: 2021 sales job + Notion templates built in bathroom/commute seams → €2k/mo → quit → later seven-figure solo path. Framed as answer to Ghost Protocol readers who cannot “disappear.”

## Why it matters

Anti-hustle time system for constrained calendars: progress without cannibalizing relationships or sleep. Useful frame for any side research, writing, or build habit.
