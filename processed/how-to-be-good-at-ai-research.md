# How to Be Good at AI Research

**Source:** [X Post by @adiix_official](https://x.com/adiix_official/status/2066853111763775949) (2026-06-16)

## Key Thesis

Most people optimize for **knowing more** (volume of papers, demos, threads). This is the wrong variable.

The field is a firehose. The real skill is building systems to filter, synthesize, and act on information effectively.

## Why It Matters

This aligns with vault principles around knowledge graphs, second-brain systems, and compounding intelligence rather than raw consumption.
