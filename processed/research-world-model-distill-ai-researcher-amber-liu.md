# How to Distill a $10M+ AI Researcher

**Source:** [X Article @JIACHENLIU8](https://x.com/jiachenliu8/status/2074558254328455577)  
**Deep dive:** [Research World Model blog](https://www.agenticresearch.sh/blog/research-world-model) · [ARA-Labs code](https://github.com/ARA-Labs/Agent-Native-Research-Artifact)

## Claim

**Research process > research outcomes.** Elite intuition is preserved, retrievable **process knowledge** — not magic — but papers and repos discard failed pivots and "why we stopped" traces.

## Research World Model

Explicit, queryable cognitive archive across sessions: laws, recipes, failure states. Mirrors scientist loop (explore/hypothesize/act/observe/reflect).

ARC-AGI-3 ls20 benchmark (~1% frontier pass): without archive, agent wandered 5,000+ steps; with world model, **63-step** solve using accumulated mechanics map.

## Why it matters

Parallels OKF/wiki compounding and trace-mining theses: science quality depends on **infrastructure that retains process**, not only polished outputs. Architectural path to "AI as persistent research system" vs one-shot answer generation.
