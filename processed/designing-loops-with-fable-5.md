# Designing loops with Fable 5

Lance Martin's practical tips for Mythos-class models.

## Key Takeaways
- Self-correction loops (via /goal or Outcomes) outperform self-critique because grading happens in an independent context window.
- Memory works best as a 5-step progression the model manages itself.
- Fable 5 shows stronger structural experimentation and higher verification coverage than prior models.

Relevant to Hermes prompting and goal-execution with Fable 5.
