# Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP)

Anthropic's RSP is a public commitment that pairs increasingly powerful model
deployment with a ladder of safety controls. Each rung is an AI Safety Level
(see [[ai-safety-levels]]) gated by capability evaluations and corresponding
mitigation requirements (security, deployment safeguards, evaluation cadence).
