# /make-requirements-great

George's skill for disciplined PRD reviews with AI.

## Core Rules
- Requirements have three altitudes (business, stakeholder, solution)
- Weasel-word scan (quantify or remove)
- Catalogue-level checks (consistency, completeness, traceability)
- Flag open decisions instead of inventing plausible values

Relevant to product management and skill authoring.
