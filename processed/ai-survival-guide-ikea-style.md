# AI Survival Guide (IKEA-Style)

**Source:** Ruben Hassid (@rubenhassid). A 6-level guide from zero to building apps, written in deadpan IKEA-instruction style. Core audience: non-technical users.

## The 6 Levels

| Level | What | Key Trick |
|-------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Claude.ai | Opus 4.8 + Thinking:High + AskUserQuestion |
| 2 | Voice | Wispr Flow — talk 4x faster than you type |
| 3 | Cowork | Desktop app, creates real files in your folder |
| 4 | Connectors | Gmail + Calendar + meeting notes for context |
| 5 | Skills | `/skill-creator` — teach Claude your workflows |
| 6 | Code | `/` tab — build websites/apps with English |

## The One-Line Trick

> "Help me do [X] for [Y]. Use AskUserQuestion first."

Claude asks 3-5 questions. You click answers. The more you say, the better the output. This one pattern works across all 6 levels — from simple prompts to app building.

## Key Principle

AI just needs context. It needs to know you. Connect your apps, give it your files, teach it your preferences through skills. The bar to knowing is trying — most people never even tried.
