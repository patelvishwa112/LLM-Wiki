# The Untrainable

Sarah Guo's thesis on where value survives as models improve.

## The Core Claim
Intelligence keeps getting cheaper. Value slides toward the few places a model can't reach: private ground truth, relationships, accountability, and the slow work of translating a firm's reality so a model can act on it.

Relevant to career strategy, investing, and enterprise AI.
