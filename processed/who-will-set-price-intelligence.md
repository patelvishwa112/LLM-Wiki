# Who Will Set the Price of Intelligence?

Jaya Gupta (Foundation Capital) argues that AI reorganizes markets around **who controls the price of intelligence** — labs, applications, and nation-states are all bidding for allocation rights.

## Why AI Investing ≠ Software Investing

SaaS metrics (CAC, NDR, Rule of 40) sat on stable forces: distribution cost and switching cost. AI adds **more variables**, **uneven coupling**, and **sub-curves** (reasoning, context, tool use, etc.) that reprice each other hourly. The model is a moving equation, not a fixed underwriting template.

## Variables That Matter

| Bucket | Examples | Strategic effect |
|--------|----------|------------------|
| Familiar | Capability, cost, latency | Moves bottlenecks; cost shocks reroute margin pools (DeepSeek as pricing war) |
| New curves | Inference-time compute, task horizon, AI-in-its-own-production | Depth × length drives token bills; progress becomes endogenous and faster than fundraising cycles |
| External | Geopolitics, deployment topology, talent | Open-weight vs closed frontier; cloud/edge/on-device splits context ownership; researchers reshape the cost-capability curve |

**Inference-time compute** is the application-layer counterattack: spend tokens only where outcomes justify cost, vs labs pushing usage expansion.

## Layer Migration as Default Risk

Platform dependency kills in SaaS were the exception; in AI they are the norm. Value jumps into the model, open weights, customer data platforms, agent runtimes, or devices when any variable shifts. Buyer sophistication is catching up to vendor lock-in arbitrage.

## Company Building Inverted

- Enterprise needs workflow-embedded context; frontier API ≠ enterprise product.
- Outcomes + services economics split investors (product vs services-as-software).
- Winning pattern: tight feedback loops, small empowered teams, reorganize when the curve moves — not six-month plans.

## Trading-Book Mental Model

Durable AI companies (and countries) must know which curves they are **long** and **short**, recover fast from wrong bets, and balance **safe enough to trust** with **cheap enough to spread** — without ceding the future to closed labs or rival states alone.
