# How Anthropic's Finance Team Uses Claude

**Source:** Alice Fong, Anthropic Corporate Finance & Strategy team. Uses Claude Cowork and Claude for Excel in parallel.

## The Core Problem

Finance professionals spend most of their time verifying numbers are right — re-reading decks after every refresh, checking commentary against latest figures — instead of analyzing what the numbers mean. Claude holds the "integrity layer" so human time goes to narrative and judgment.

## Key Workflows

| Workflow | Cadence | Tool | What Claude Does |
|----------|---------|------|-----------------|
| Board deck | Quarterly | Claude Cowork | Reconciles numbers across slides, checks narrative consistency, flags contradictions and missing context |
| Monthly financial review | Monthly | Claude Cowork | Drafts first-pass commentary in established voice against forecast variance |
| Financial model work | As needed | Claude for Excel | Traces references across tabs, diagnoses structural issues, summarizes key drivers |
| Cross-team context | Continuous | Google Workspace & Slack | Pulls decisions and reasoning from docs, email, and Slack |

## How Context Drives Results

- Project memory: commit documents that matter, Claude recalls them when relevant
- Separate projects for separate audiences: monthly review vs. board deck have different tone and conventions
- Claude for Excel evolved from single-tab to cross-tab reference tracing, finding root causes in balance sheets

## Across the CFO Organization

| Function | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| **Finance & Strategy** | Interactive forecasting dashboards built from prompts; daily revenue/metrics digest in Slack |
| **Accounting** | GL-to-subledger reconciliations, bank recs, flux analysis on all three financial statements |
| **Corporate Development & IR** | Screening reports for 3–4 acquisition targets/day from notes and public data |
| **Tax & Treasury** | Transfer pricing, R&D credits, nexus questions with primary-source citations |

## Advice for Getting Started

Start simple — ask Claude to read a doc and summarize — then push boundaries. Most valuable on recurring workflows where consistency compounds and project memory gets richer every pass. No elaborate stack needed: Claude Cowork projects, Claude for Excel, and Google Suite Connector are sufficient.
