# A Framework for Frontier AI and the Dawning of a New Age — Demis Hassabis

Thesis: AGI (full human cognitive capabilities) is **likely only a few short years away** — “foothills of the singularity,” impact ~10× Industrial Revolution at 10× speed. Upside: science, energy, materials, abundance. Stance: **cautious optimism** while commercial/geopolitical races outpace understanding.

## Risk framing

- Present: cybersecurity challenges from frontier models
- Near: nuclear / bio capability risks
- Horizon: control of **agentic, recursively self-improving** systems; unknown unknowns
- Diagnosis: field is not currently giving itself time/space to get the next step right

## Proposal — Frontier AI Standards Body (US-first)

Modeled on a federally overseen public-private / self-regulatory org (**FINRA-like**):

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Board | Independent technical experts + open-source representation |
| Funding | Substantial; largely industry; talent + compute for testing |
| Partners | Federal agencies, National Labs; later third-party auditor ecosystem |
| **Frontier-class** | Thresholds on dynamic benchmarks (updated ~quarterly; deprecate saturated) |
| **Frontier Labs** | Orgs holding such models; prestige designation open to any org that hits bar |

**Process:** voluntary share up to **30 days pre-release** → once protocols proven, **mandatory pass for US deployment**; post-release vuln remediation with the Body.

**Eval content:** cyber, bio, high-risk domains; agentic tests for guardrail bypass / deception; watermarking; human-readable reasoning tokens.

**Independence path:** start with lab consultation → held-out tests owned by the Body to reduce overfitting.

**Scope:** open or closed, any country of origin, if frontier-class; **startups/academia non-frontier exempt**. Can ratchet (including coordinating a development slowdown among Frontier Labs). Intended as seed for **international** consensus.

Best practices for labs: model cards, internal cyber, personnel vetting, safety/security research resourcing.

## Beyond tech

Even with technical risk managed: post-scarcity economics, values, meaning/purpose, human condition — not for technologists alone.

## Why it matters

Canonical 2026 lab-leader governance blueprint: capability definition of AGI, race dynamics, and a concrete institutional design (benchmarks + pre-deployment gate) that sits between pure voluntaryism and heavy statute.
