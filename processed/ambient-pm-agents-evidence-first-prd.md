# Ambient PM agents

George (prodmgmt.world) proposes **ambient agents** attached to early product ideas — daily/cron sweeps of support, sales, Slack, analytics, research — building **evidence packets** (pro + counter signal) *before* roadmap or PRD commitment.

## Packet contents

Idea definition, non-goals, supporting/weakening evidence, assumptions, concerned stakeholders, smallest test, eng path, timing risks.

## Review model

Stakeholder review like **GitHub PR** on the packet; agents explain implications per function. PM still owns strategy calls (build / defer / kill).

## Anti-pattern

**Document trap** — AI volume mistaken for decision quality. Success metric = uncertainty stays visible (raw evidence, labeled assumptions, dissent), not polished spec completeness.

## Harness

Needs permissions, audit trail, evidence repo — not a single PRD prompt. Points to **PM OS** as starter stack.
