# how to be good at research

vivek's practical field guide to the actual skills behind research.

## Core Skills
- Pick your own problems (reverse-engineer from outcomes you want to exist)
- Upgrade your inputs (old material + range across subfields)
- Write everything down (Feynman's rule + Darwin's habit)
- Tighten the loop (tooling + volume of experiments)
- Stare at the outputs (raw data + failures, not just curves)
- Wander on purpose (breadth as insurance)
- Find your people (open doors + generosity)

Relevant to the user's career transition and research habits.
