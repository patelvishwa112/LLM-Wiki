# AI Content System — From Ideas to 5M Impressions

## Summary
A framework for building an AI-powered content system that finds your ideas, drafts them in your voice, publishes them, and learns from what works. The system drove 5M impressions in 2 weeks and 100K bookmarks in 2 months.

## Key Takeaways

- **End-to-end pipeline:** Idea discovery → drafting in your voice → publishing → learning from performance
- **Agent-driven:** Designed to be handed off to an AI agent for execution
- **Results:** 5M impressions in 2 weeks, 100K bookmarks in 2 months
- **Voice preservation:** The system learns and maintains the creator's unique voice, not generic AI content
- **Feedback loop:** Learns from what works to improve future content
