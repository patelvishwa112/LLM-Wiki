## Installation (30-Second Version)

- **Claude Code:** Drop skill folder into `~/.claude/skills/`, restart. Plugin repos use `/plugin` or `npx`.
- **Claude.ai / Cowork:** Download .zip or SKILL.md → Customize → Skills → "+" → Upload.
- **Safety:** Ask Claude to audit unfamiliar repos before installing.

## Bottom Line

The model race is mostly over. The new question is "how do I stop re-explaining myself?" Skills are the answer. **Start with 3:** Superpowers (build), interview-me (clarity), skill-creator (make your own). The gap between skill-users and non-users will get embarrassing fast.

**Skip:** Caveman — debunked, only trims tokens on final output, not the thinking where real cost lives.
