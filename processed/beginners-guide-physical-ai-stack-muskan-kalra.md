# The Beginner’s Guide to Physical AI Stack

Investor/operator primer by **Muskan Kalra** (@muskan_kalra24, ex-Vana ecosystem / Polygon DevRel). Framing: Physical AI capital is booming (**$16.3B Q1 2026**, **>$23B by late May 2026**); headlines feel chaotic until mapped as a **stack**.

## Scope

Physical AI ≠ only humanoids. Includes industrial arms, warehouse robots, AVs, ag machinery, drones, surgical robots, quadrupeds, etc. ~542k industrial robots installed 2024; ~4.7M operational fleet. Humanoids are the **complete stress test** (perception, balance, manipulation, power, safety, learning) — useful case study, not the whole market.

## Five layers

### 1. Brain (AI / intelligence)
- VLA (Vision-Language-Action): see + instruction → action tokens; often atop low-level balance/force controllers.
- Frontier: generalist brain across tasks and bodies.
- Watch: Figure Helix (vertical), Skild AI ($1.4B raise, >$14B val, omni-bodied), Physical Intelligence (π policies), NVIDIA GR00T, DeepMind Gemini Robotics + Apptronik, Genesis AI.

### 2. Body (humanoid OEMs)
- ~13k global humanoid shipments 2025 (Omdia) — research/pilots still dominate.
- US: Figure ($39B, BMW), Tesla Optimus (internal), Apptronik Apollo (>$935M Series A; Mercedes, GXO, Jabil), Agility Digit (GXO; $2.5B SPAC), 1X NEO home ($20k early).
- China: Unitree G1 (~$13.5k cost leader; IPO path), AgiBot (top 2025 shipper ~5k), UBTech Walker industrial.
- Also: NEURA 4NE1, Boston Dynamics Atlas → industrial (Hyundai, DeepMind 2026).

### 3. Parts (actuators, gears, hands, sensors)
- 40+ actuated joints; bottlenecks: harmonic reducers, motors, roller screws, tactile sensing, thermal, reliability.
- Gearboxes: Harmonic Drive, Nabtesco, LeaderDrive. Actuators: Sanhua, Tuopu, Inovance. Hands: Sharpa (GR00T Wave), Wonik, etc. Perception: Orbbec, RoboSense, Hesai. Force: ATI/Novanta, Vishay. Compute: Jetson Thor. Batteries: CATL, Samsung SDI, LGES, Panasonic.

### 4. Fuel (training data)
- No public “robot internet”; four collection modes: **teleop**, **sim** (sim-to-real gap), **human video/wearables**, **fleet data** (long-term moat).
- Watch: XDOF ($70M teleop infra), Mecka AI ($60M human motion), Genesis, PI, NVIDIA Isaac/Omniverse; body makers collecting fleets.

### 5. Proving grounds (deployments)
- Scaled ROI today is mostly **non-humanoid**: Amazon >1M robots; Symbotic ~$22.3B backlog (Walmart-heavy).
- Humanoid pilots: Figure@BMW (1,250+ hours, linked to 30k+ vehicles), Apptronik commercial pilots, Agility multi-customer ops, UBTech industrial China.
- Trap: pilot ≠ scaled fleet; purpose-built automation still prints the money.

## One-breath map

Brain decides → body carries → parts move → data teaches → deployment proves reliability and economics. Capital floods all five layers at once; the stack is coherent even when news is not.
