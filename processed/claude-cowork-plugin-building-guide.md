# Building Claude Cowork Plugins That Actually Work

## Summary
A complete guide to building Claude Cowork plugins — encoded expertise that turns a chatbot into a personal AI job role. A plugin is not a prompt; it is a job description, an SOP, templates, and a quality bar bundled together. The mental shift is from conversing to delegating.

## Key Takeaways
- **Mental shift**: A prompt is a conversation. A plugin is a job role — encoded expertise that does the work while you do something else
- **When to build a plugin**: Task repeats at least twice a month, the process is stable enough to write down, and the output has a defined shape
- **Plugin anatomy**: Role description, standard operating procedure (SOP), templates, and quality bar
- **Real impact**: A four-hour content job shipped in eleven minutes after building a plugin
