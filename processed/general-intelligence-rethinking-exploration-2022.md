# General Intelligence Requires Rethinking Exploration (2022)

## Summary
@willccbb revisits a 2022 paper (arxiv.org/abs/2211.07819) that feels remarkably timely in 2026. The paper argues we're at the cusp of transitioning from "learning from data" to "learning what data to learn from" as the central focus of AI research — a shift now materializing through self-teaching and recursive improvement.

## Key Takeaways
- **Paper:** "General Intelligence Requires Rethinking Exploration" (arXiv:2211.07819, 2022)
- **Core thesis:** Moving from "learning from data" to "learning what data to learn from" is the next frontier
- **Prescience:** The paper anticipated the current wave of self-teaching and exploration-focused AI research by 4 years
- **Timing:** Now feels like it could "anchor the next wave of innovation in self-teaching"
