# On-Policy Distillation Resources (2026)

## Summary
2026 is shaping up to be the year of On-Policy Distillation (OPD), specifically On-Policy Student Distillation (OPSD). This note collects the key resources surfaced by @neural_avb for getting up to speed.

## Key Takeaways
- OPD/OPSD is a major trend in 2026 for training smaller models from larger teacher models
- HuggingFace has a dedicated on-policy distillation space (HuggingFaceH4/on-policy-distillation)
- TRL (Transformer Reinforcement Learning) now has a distillation trainer for hands-on work
- Multi-teacher OPD is an emerging variant worth watching
- Recommended reading order: Thinking Machines blog → HuggingFace space → TRL trainer → multi-teacher resources
