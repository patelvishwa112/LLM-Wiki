# Opus in Cybersecurity: Partner Deployments

**Source:** Anthropic, June 2026. Partners building defensive cyber offerings on Claude Opus.

## Three Pillars of AI-Powered Defense

1. **Continuous offensive testing at production scale** — attack your own systems like an adversary
2. **Closing the gap between finding and fixing** — triage, prioritization, patch testing, and remediation as one loop
3. **Getting AI into production, governed** — deployment, governance, and audit evidence for agentic security

## Partner Results

| Partner | Offering | Key Result |
|---------|----------|------------|
| **Wiz** | Red Agent | Continuous pentesting across 150K+ assets/week, thousands of high/critical findings, zero false positives |
| **Palo Alto Networks** | Unit 42 Frontier AI Defense | Year's pentesting effort in <3 weeks; maps vulnerability chains into critical attack paths |
| **Accenture** | Cyber.AI | Coverage from ~10% to 80%+ across 1,600 apps / 500K+ APIs; scan turnaround 3–5 days → <1 hour |
| **CrowdStrike** | Frontier AI Readiness & Resilience | Opus + AI Red Team for continuous zero-day hunting in customer apps |
| **Deloitte** | CTEM (built on Ascend) | Discovery → validation → prioritization → remediation as single workflow; hours not days |
| **Trend Micro** | Vision One / TrendAI | Opus-assisted vuln research; virtual patching up to 96 days before vendor patch |
| **PwC** | Claude Native Cybersecurity | Secure AI Adoption (sandbox to production in weeks) + Scaled Frontier Defense |
