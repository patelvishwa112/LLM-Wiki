# Build Your AI-Powered Morning Brief

**Source:** [X Post by Dave Kline](https://x.com/dklineii/status/2068331296032424433)

This X post provides a complete, ready-to-implement skills file for an **AI Chief of Staff** manifested as a daily **Morning Brief**.

## Core Philosophy
Most managers start the day reactively. Effective leaders use a consistent 15-minute morning block (before inbox) where AI synthesizes calendar, tasks, email, chat, metrics, and 1:1 notes into a focused brief.

## The Six Reads (in order)
1. **The Day** — Schedule + due items + open blocks
2. **The Decisions** — Blocked items prioritized by cost of delay
3. **The People** — Team attention needs (1:1s, quiet members, load carriers)
4. **The Meetings** — One-line prep: why it exists, desired outcome, key question
5. **The World** — 3-5 relevant external signals (clients, competitors, industry)
6. **The Move** — Highest-leverage action + drafted message toward north star

## Four Rules
- Never make theoretical suggestions — name specific meetings, times, people
- Never stop because something is missing — flag gaps and continue
- Never take shortcuts — do the research
- Never make anything up — be explicit about unknowns

## Six Prompts Provided
The post includes six concrete prompts for LLMs (Claude etc.) covering full brief generation, people radar, meeting prep + research, world scan, day grading against north star, and closing loops.

This system is designed to be copied into an LLM to become a recurring overnight Chief of Staff skill.
