# HRM-Text: 1B Parameter Reasoning Model

## Summary
HRM-Text by Sapient Intelligence is an ultra-lean 1B-parameter reasoning language model that achieves competitive performance with ~1/1000th the training data of comparable models. Trained on just 40B structured tokens in roughly one day on a $1,000 budget.

## Key Takeaways
- **1B parameters** with strong general reasoning performance
- **40B structured tokens** — approximately 1/1000 of typical training data for comparable models
- **~1 day training** on a $1,000 budget makes experimentation radically cheaper
- Opens the door to testing theories and research concepts previously deemed too expensive
- Part of a new paradigm for powerful, accessible, and easily adaptable AI
