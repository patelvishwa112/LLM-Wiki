# Encoder-Free Models and the Bitter Lesson We Keep Seeing

**Source:** [X Post by @rumik_ai](https://x.com/rumik_ai/status/2066984970783166907) (2026-06-16)

## Key Thesis

Sutton's Bitter Lesson repeats across modalities: we repeatedly hand-design complex structures (encoders, codecs) to feed data into models, only for scale + simpler architectures to eventually win.

## Why It Matters

This is a core pattern in modern ML. Understanding where hand-engineering is being replaced by scale is valuable for both research direction and system design.
