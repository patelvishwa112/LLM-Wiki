# Zen and the Art of AI Research

**Author:** Jack Morris (@jxmnop)  
**Format:** X Article (high-engagement career/research advice)

## Core Thesis
Getting started in AI research doesn't require formal training. The practical path is a combination of:

- (i) Reading papers and code deeply
- (ii) Writing (notes, blogs, code, papers)
- (iii) Talking to people in the field

This simple loop compounds into research capability.
