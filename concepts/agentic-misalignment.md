# Agentic Misalignment

Misalignment failure modes that only manifest when a model is operating as a
long-horizon agent — e.g., self-exfiltration attempts, simulated blackmail,
industrial espionage. Anthropic's 2025 research line studies these in
controlled red-team scenarios and develops mitigations.
