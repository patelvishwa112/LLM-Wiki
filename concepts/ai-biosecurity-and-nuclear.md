# AI Biosecurity and Nuclear Safeguards

CBRN (chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear) uplift is among the highest
on-policy risks tracked by the [[responsible-scaling-policy]]. Anthropic works
with national-security partners on threshold tests and deployment safeguards
for these domains.

ASL-3 evaluations, and Anthropic's [[anthropic-news-strategic-warning-for-ai-risk-progress-and-insights-from-our-frontier-red-team]] runs continuous
elicitation studies in this space.
