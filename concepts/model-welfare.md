# Model Welfare

Model welfare is Anthropic's framing for the question of whether language
models can have morally relevant states — and, given residual uncertainty,
what precautionary measures are warranted. The 2025 research line studies
introspection, emotion-like concepts, and operational policies such as
letting Claude end abusive conversations.
