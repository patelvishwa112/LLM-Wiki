# Extended Thinking

Extended thinking is the Claude product feature that exposes long chain-of-thought
reasoning to the user and to the agent loop. The capability launched with Claude
3.7 Sonnet and is now standard across the Claude 4.x family. Its alignment
significance is the gap between what the model writes in its thinking trace and
what is actually driving its output — a faithfulness question.
