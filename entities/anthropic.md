# Anthropic

AI safety company founded in 2021 by [[dario-amodei]], Daniela Amodei, and
other former OpenAI researchers. Develops the [[claude-models|Claude]] family
of LLMs, the [[claude-code]] CLI, [[model-context-protocol|MCP]], and
publishes the [[transformer-circuits-thread]] and the
[[anthropic-economic-index]].

## Foundational positioning

- [[anthropic-news-core-views-on-ai-safety]] — Anthropic's "Core Views on
  AI Safety" articulates the company's bet: powerful AI is coming and we do
  not yet know how to make it safe
