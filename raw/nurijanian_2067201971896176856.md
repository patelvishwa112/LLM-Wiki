# /opportunity: an AI PM OS workflow to turn chaos into order

**George from prodmgmt.world (@nurijanian)** — Jun 17, 2026

## Thesis

RICE and similar scoring break at the **opportunity** stage (before solutions are known). Product strategy = choosing which opportunities to explore, not sequencing features with fake precision.

Workflow chains three steps from Teresa Torres's **Opportunity Solution Tree** (Continuous Discovery Habits).

## Why scoring fails upstream

RICE assumes known solutions (reach, impact, confidence, effort). At opportunity stage: effort unknown, reach requires research scoring was meant to prioritize, impact becomes post-hoc justification. Spreadsheets end debate before real trade-offs.

## Step 1: Intake

Force inputs into shape before drawing nodes.

- **Outcome:** measurable behavior/business result, no solution baked in (e.g. not "build notifications center" but "increase return within 48h after relevant event").
- **Journey nodes:** moments in time (Discover, Decide, Onboard, Use, Review)—not categories (Reliability, Trust). Categories inherit org chart; moments expose customer sequence and gaps.

## Step 2: Tree builder

Outcome → opportunities → solutions. Opportunities = **needs/pains/desires** in user voice, never solutions ("can't export PDF" → reframe to underlying need).

Structural checks:
- **Distinctness** among siblings
- **Parent-child** validity; delete generic parent with only one child
- Optional **MECE** pass on a slice

Evidence on nodes: quotes, frequency, confidence. Hypotheses logged separately from heard needs.

## Step 3: Selection (no spreadsheet)

Four qualitative lenses (Torres): opportunity sizing, market factors, company factors, customer factors (importance × dissatisfaction). **Comparative prose**, not weighted scores. Question: "which is most important to address **right now**?" not "should we address this?"

Rules: no effort estimates yet; treat as reversible exploration commitment; pick from crummy draft, revise every 3–4 interviews.

Output: winner + rationale, key unknowns, next interview questions.

## AI role

Enforcement of discipline at scale—reframe solution-flavored quotes, dedupe snippets, link evidence, MECE/overlap flags. Author claims week of synthesis → afternoon with Claude running PM OS `/opportunity` workflow (intake, tree, MECE, four-factor selection). One of 11 workflows in PM OS.
