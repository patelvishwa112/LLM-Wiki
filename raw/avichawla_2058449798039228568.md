# Build Human-Like Memory for Your Agents — Graphiti

By Avi Chawla (@_avichawla) | May 24, 2026 | 10K views | 137 bookmarks

Graphiti is an open-source (26K+ stars) knowledge graph for agents that evolves continuously and is temporally-aware.

## Architecture (3 subgraphs)

1. **Episode subgraph**: Captures raw data with timestamps, retaining every detail for easy historical lookup.
2. **Semantic entity subgraph**: Extracts entities (e.g., "Alice," "Google") and facts ("Alice works at Google"). Everything versioned — outdated info gets replaced.
3. **Community subgraph**: Groups related entities into clusters, with summaries for faster retrieval.

## Performance
- 18.5% higher accuracy vs MemGPT
- 90% lower latency vs MemGPT
- Fully open-source
