# Introducing the Anthropic Economic Advisory Council

**Category:** News
**URL:** https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-the-anthropic-economic-advisory-council
**Ingested:** 2026-05-22

Today, we’re announcing the formation of the Anthropic Economic Advisory Council, a group of distinguished economists who will provide Anthropic with expert guidance on the economic implications of AI development and deployment. The Council will advise Anthropic on AI's impact on labor markets, economic growth, and broader socioeconomic systems.
