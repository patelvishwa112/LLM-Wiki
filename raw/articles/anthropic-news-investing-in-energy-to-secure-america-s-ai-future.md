# Investing in energy to secure America's AI future

**Category:** News
**URL:** https://www.anthropic.com/news/investing-in-energy-to-secure-america-s-ai-future
**Ingested:** 2026-05-22

Energy is central to winning the AI race and we need to ensure that America has the necessary infrastructure to maintain its lead. The importance of building this infrastructure goes beyond just powering data centers—the country that controls the energy to train and deploy frontier AI models will shape the future of global innovation, economic competitiveness, and democratic values.
