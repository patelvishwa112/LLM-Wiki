How to Build a Claude Cowork Plugin That Actually Works (FULL GUIDE)

Last week I shipped a four-hour content job in eleven minutes. Not because I got faster. Because three weeks earlier I spent one evening building a plugin, and the plugin now does the work while I do something else.

Anatomy of a Plugin: role description, standard operating procedure, templates, quality bar.
