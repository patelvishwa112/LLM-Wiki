# Why On-Policy Distillation Works and Naive Self-Distillation Doesn't

**Rishabh Tiwari (@rish2k1)** posted a detailed technical analysis (June 20, 2026) on distillation for reasoning models.

## Core Argument

**On-policy distillation** works well when the teacher is both *better on reward* and *close to the student*. It provides a dense token-by-token signal that is effectively KL-regularized RL toward a reward-tilted policy. When the teacher puts more probability on higher-reward trajectories (larger same-family model or RL expert), distillation is very sample- and compute-efficient.

**Naive self-distillation** fails systematically. The self-distillation “teacher” (student conditioned on privileged information such as gold answers, reference solutions, or prior-attempt feedback) is not a good reward-tilted target. It favors responses that *look like* they had the privileged information, not responses that actually score higher on the verifier.

## Observed Failure Modes (across chemistry MCQ, tool-use, competition math / Polaris with Qwen3-8B reasoning models)

- **Hallucination at inference**: 96–100% on training distributions, 80–98% OOD. The model fabricates references/previous solutions/feedback it never received. Becomes the dominant response template.
- **Degraded reasoning**: Epistemic verbalization tokens (“wait”, “perhaps”, “actually”, hedging/backtracking) collapse from ~86 to <10 tokens per response. RL preserves them; self-distillation strips them out, producing overconfident, non-exploratory outputs.
- **Poor OOD generalization**: In-distribution accuracy can look competitive, but OOD performance drops 6–25 points behind pure RL. The learned response shape is tightly tied to the training distribution.

Prompt engineering and loss modifications (advantage masking, RL hybridization) reduced symptoms but never fully closed the gap to pure RL on OOD metrics. Patches helped by *reducing* the influence of the pure distillation term.

## Key Takeaways

- Track more than IID accuracy: hallucination rate, epistemic verbalization count, and OOD generalization.
- Self-distillation is not hopeless — approaches that keep privileged information in fast/slow weight setups (FST/GEPA) or use partial oracle solutions during RL (POPE-style) avoid these pitfalls.
- Pure RL remained the strongest overall method for robust reasoning models in their experiments.
