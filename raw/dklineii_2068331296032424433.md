# Build Your AI-Powered Morning Brief

**Post by @dklineii (Dave Kline)**

This is a detailed "skills file" for building an **AI Chief of Staff** in the form of a daily **Morning Brief**.

### Core Philosophy
Most managers start the day reactively (phone → email → chaos). Effective leaders prepare first. Kline’s system is a consistent 15-minute morning block (before touching your inbox) where AI reads your calendar, tasks, email, chat, metrics, and 1:1 notes and delivers a focused brief.

The brief is built around **Six Reads** (in order):

1. **The Day** — Combined view of schedule + due items + open blocks.
2. **The Decisions** — Everything currently blocked on you, prioritized by cost of delay.
3. **The People** — Who on your team needs attention today (1:1 commitments, people who’ve gone quiet, who’s carrying a load).
4. **The Meetings** — One-line prep for every meeting (why it exists, outcome you want, the one key question to ask).
5. **The World** — 3–5 genuinely relevant external signals (clients, competitors, industry moves) that matter to your team.
6. **The Move** — The single highest-leverage change you can make today to advance your north star (specific action + drafted message).

### Four Non-Negotiable Rules for the Brief
- Never make theoretical suggestions — always name specific meetings, times, and people.
- Never stop because something is missing — flag gaps and continue.
- Never take shortcuts — actually do the research.
- Never make anything up — be explicit about unknowns.

He provides six specific prompts you can hand to an LLM (Claude, etc.) to build the full system:

- Prompt 1: Build the full brief
- Prompt 2: People radar (the highest-leverage read most people skip)
- Prompt 3: Meeting prep + external research
- Prompt 4: World scan (relevant signals only)
- Prompt 5: Grade the day against your north star and recommend changes
- Prompt 6: Close yesterday’s loops and draft obvious replies

The entire article is designed to be copied into an LLM so it can turn itself into your recurring overnight Chief of Staff skill.
