# Build Your Own LLM — free workshop materials

**Author:** @JustinAngel  
**Posted:** June 23, 2026

## Announcement

Justin released full recordings and materials from the in-person workshop **"Build Your Own LLM"** — free, no math or ML prerequisites required.

**Includes:**
- 23 videos
- 250-page slide deck
- 50 hands-on Excel + coding exercises

## Links

- **Videos (YouTube playlist):** https://go.JustinAngel.ai/playlist
- **Slide deck:** https://go.JustinAngel.ai/deck
- **Exercises (Drive):** https://go.JustinAngel.ai/drive

## Workshop outline (23 sections)

1. Sampling LLMs (temperature, top-p, top-k)
2. Reverse engineering LLMs (GPT-2 small)
3. Perceptrons (wx+b)
4. Activation functions (ReLU, GELU, SwiGLU)
5. GPU coding (PyTorch, torch.compile, CUDA, Triton)
6. MLPs / FFNs
7. Loss functions (RMSE, cross entropy, loss landscapes)
8. Backpropagation (training loops, optimizers, LR, batch size)
9. Saving & loading models (incl. Hugging Face)
10. Initialization (Kaiming, Glorot)
11. Residuals (add, scale, gate, concat)
12. Normalization (RMSNorm, LayerNorm, etc.)
13. Regularization (dropout, gradient clipping, weight decay)
14. Softmax
15. Tokenizers (BPE, SentencePiece, etc.)
16. Embeddings (absolute vs learned, RoPE)
17. Attention (MHA, GQA, MQA, MLA)
18. Transformers
19. Pre-training (data sources, cleaning, filtering)
20. Evaluation (benchmarks, LLM-as-judge, etc.)
21. Instruction tuning (Alpaca, Self-Instruct)
22. Reinforcement learning (SimPO from scratch)
23. What we didn't cover (scaling, inference optimization, safety)

Builds an LLM step-by-step from fundamentals through pre-training, instruction tuning, and RL.
