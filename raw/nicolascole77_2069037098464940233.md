# The Faster You Master These 5 Skills, The Sooner You'll Hit $10,000 Per Month As A Writer

**Nicolas Cole** (@Nicolascole77) — X Article, Jun 2026.

---

Here's a question I get asked all the time: "Cole, how quickly can I become a Premium Ghostwriter? I want to start landing high-paying clients FAST."

You need to master **5 hard skills** before you can charge above **$2,000+ per month**. Being a "good" writer isn't one of them.

## 1. Project management & client leadership

Freelance writers act like employees waiting for instructions. Premium ghostwriters act like **1-person business owners**: guide the client, walk them through process, over-communicate, reassure.

## 2. Content strategy & topic development

Value starts **before** writing. Help clients identify topics from credibility, personal experiences, unique industry insights; organize ideas for the target reader.

## 3. Writing in other people's voices — 5 archetypes

- **Storyteller** — "Back when I was building my first company…"
- **Opinionator** — "I just think it's crazy that…"
- **Fact Presenter** — data/research citations
- **Frameworker** — step-by-step logic
- **F-Bomber** — expletives / controversial punch

Recognize archetype and replicate style.

## 4. Specialization (platform or service)

LinkedIn ≠ X ≠ newsletters ≠ paid newsletters. Pick a platform; become the go-to ghostwriter for it.

## 5. Scaling with technology & curiosity

Scale client content library with tools/AI; stay adaptable as platforms and models change.
